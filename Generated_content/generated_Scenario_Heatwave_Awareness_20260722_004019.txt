The midday sun was already beating down, and Alex felt the familiar tug of impatience as the clock ticked toward lunch. The team was behind schedule, and the heat made every movement feel heavier. Alex glanced at the scaffolding, knowing the next inspection had to be done before the afternoon shift change, but the thought of climbing in this heat made frustration bubble up. Trying to push through, Alex skipped the planned water break, telling themselves they could catch up later. As they started the climb, the warmth seemed to fog their focus — a loose bolt caught Alex’s eye, but the urgency to finish clouded their judgment, and they moved past it without a second look.
