**Heatwave Safety Snapshot**

- **Temperature Impact**: Heat drains energy, clouds focus, and slows reactions — turning small oversights into big risks.
- **Human Factors Amplified**: Warm weather fuels fatigue, frustration, and rushed decisions, making “eyes not on task” and “mind not on task” far more likely.
- **Key Risks**: Dehydration, lost concentration, and irritability all rise together, increasing the chance of missed hazards or unsafe shortcuts.
- **Safety Focus**: July is Heat Illness Awareness Month — a prime time to reinforce hydration, scheduled breaks, and pre-task checks to counter heat’s hidden effects on performance.
