**Infographic: School Reopening Safety Awareness**

- **Traffic Changes:** Expect busier roads as school resumes—maintain safe following distances, especially near school zones.
- **Morning Rush:** Tight schedules can lead to distraction; pause before driving to reset your focus and recharge.
- **Family Safety:** Use positive language when discussing safety with teens to encourage responsible behavior.
- **Habit Building:** Consistent safe practices, like good lighting and awareness, protect families day and night.
- **Stay Engaged:** Recognize when you’re feeling rushed or frustrated, and take a moment to reconnect before starting tasks.
