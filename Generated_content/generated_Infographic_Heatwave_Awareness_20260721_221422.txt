- **Heat Impacts Focus**: High temperatures reduce concentration and decision-making ability, increasing the chance of overlooking hazards.  
- **Fatigue Grows Faster**: Working in heat drains energy more quickly, leading to quicker mental and physical exhaustion.  
- **Rushing and Complacency Rise**: Discomfort from heat can make workers skip safety steps or hurry to finish tasks.  
- **RYS Checks Matter**: Regularly checking your physical and mental state helps catch early signs of heat stress before it affects safety.  
- **Simple Actions Help**: Staying hydrated, taking breaks in shade, and monitoring coworkers are key steps to stay safe during heatwaves.
