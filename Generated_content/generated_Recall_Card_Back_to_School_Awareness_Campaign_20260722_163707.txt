As the back-to-school season kicks off, our mornings become a whirlwind of schedules, traffic, and competing priorities. This shift can easily pull your focus away from safety, especially when driving. The Stay Focused, Stay Safe campaign this August is all about building one simple habit to protect yourself and others. Pick a habit—like keeping a safe following distance or pausing for a quick Rate Your State (RYS) check before you start your engine—and practice it every time you get in your vehicle. Tracking your progress through YOUFactors will help turn that habit into second nature, keeping you safe amid the busy days ahead.
